Hose Management Services

True-Blue hose management portfolio is designed to optimize asset value, mitigate the costs of hose failure, and guarantee safe operations of equipment.

Our top-of-the-line Ecoline Uniflex crimping machines are accessorized with computerized integrated quality control and quality enhancing features such as Pressure Force Monitoring (PFM), and Pressure Force Control (PFC). We can record, monitor, and assure the quality of every crimped hose. The ability to block the machine’s user interface against erroneous input increases the transparency of the production process and enhances consistent quality.

Why Choose Our Service

We adopt Uniflex Labeling System – as hose labeling technology with the following advantages:

  • Benefits of labels and what they can tell the user
  • Which components need to be replaced and when
  • The date of the exchange
  • The potential danger of the machine
  • The plan for Preventive maintenance
  • Minimizes cost of maintenance
  • Avoids equipment failures and long downtime
